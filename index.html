02 / THE CLUB
Manifesto

Brotherhood,
built to compete.

A football club with regional ambition. Brotherhood as foundation. Standards as the gate.

We didn't get scouted.
We got here.

Ubud Jungle Ballers is a football club with a clear ambition: to become Ubud's largest club and one of the most respected amateur sides in Bali. We're building a world-class amateur ecosystem — competitive at regional level, professionally structured, and deeply Balinese.

Some of us were born five minutes from the pitch. Some flew sixteen hours and never went back. The green doesn't ask where you're from — only if you'll run for the man next to you.

Three tenets
01
The Chameleon.
Adapt. Watch. Arrive grounded — the jungle teaches you how to play.
02
Local + Global.
Equal seats, one shirt. Balinese leadership. Open doors.
03
Standards earn brotherhood.
Brotherhood without structure stagnates. Show up. Train honestly. Earn the green.
